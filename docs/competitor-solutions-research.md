# AI Automation Platforms: Solutions & Industries Section Research

## Executive Summary

This research analyzes how leading AI automation platforms present their "Solutions" and "Industries" sections. The analysis covers 8 major competitors: Zapier, Make.com, UiPath, AutomationAnywhere, Workato, Moveworks, Ada, and Intercom. The focus is on understanding industry vertical targeting, page structure, messaging patterns, pain points addressed, and interactive UI elements.

## Key Insights & Recommendations

### Industry Vertical Strategy
- **RPA-focused platforms** (UiPath, AutomationAnywhere) have the most comprehensive industry coverage
- **iPaaS platforms** (Zapier, Make, Workato) organize primarily by department/function
- **Conversational AI platforms** (Ada, Moveworks) focus on specific industries with high support volume

### Content Structure Best Practices
1. Lead with **quantified business outcomes**
2. Include **industry-specific messaging** and wordplay
3. Provide **multiple content formats** (text, video, case studies)
4. Use **tabbed interfaces** to organize multiple use cases
5. Include **prominent customer logos** for social proof

### Pain Point Messaging
Most platforms address:
- Manual, repetitive tasks
- Integration challenges
- Scaling difficulties
- Cost reduction needs
- Customer/employee experience improvement

### CTA Strategy
- Primary: "Request demo" or "Get a demo"
- Secondary: "Start free trial" or "Learn more"
- Tertiary: "Read case study" or "Watch video"
